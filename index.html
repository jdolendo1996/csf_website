Tokenomics

Transparency at it's absolute best!

Choose Your Board!
At the start of the game you’ll be able to choose your own board! Each board will come with its own rarity and stats which can then be used ingame or traded later on our marketplace!
Anti Rug! Dev Is Doxxed
As this project is my baby and my information is publicly available you can be assured that I’ll only do what’s best for the project whilst keeping your funds safe. You can even check out my twitch profile here! I’m partnered with them too! https://twitch.tv/Sneakz
P2E & PVP Earnings!?
Our game will initially feature p2e earnings at a low rate which will allow you not only to earn but to also gain powerups and abilities! You can then use these upgrades in PVP to take on the best and really get some phat earns going! Play 2 Earn rewards will be related to the volume of PVP matches as they help the pool. More PVP, more Play 2 Earn rewards!
REAL TIME PVP STAKING
A world first! Real time PVP multiplayer staking! Players will be able to bet their CSF tokens against each other! Winner in order will get a higher % of the token pot so everyone gets a chance to play! These tokens will be taxed at a small rate to help fund the project.
Lottery
A weekly lottery will take place with 3 winners! 20% of the prize pool will go towards funding the project! We hope you win big!
Our Own Marketplace
Tired of annoying third party marketplaces stealing all the profit? So are we! We have our very own marketplace for trading our boards. Our tax rate for transactions here will be much smaller than the competition.
Token Tax Rate
All transactions will be taxed at a rate of 15%. These tokens will be used to fund the Play 2 Earn & Reflection Rewards pool to help fuel the sustainability of the game for years to come. Presale will temporarily have a higher tax % on sells as we create the token to mitigate a total dump and create a nice foundation for all of us!
Scholarships
As our game is P2E based, we are able to offer scholarships so you can get access to your managers super cool boards! These boards will have better stats and therefore a higher chance of winning in our balanced PVP matchmaking system.
